How BLESS Works

1. Detection

Micro-controlled device detects sulphation on battery plates without disassembly.

2. Pulse Application

Emits precise electronic pulses to dissolve sulphate crystals and restore active material.

3. Prevention & Maintenance

Prevents future sulphation, extends battery life 2–3×, and reduces replacement costs.
